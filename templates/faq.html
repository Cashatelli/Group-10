Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rent a car?

You can rent a car by visiting our booking page or contacting us directly over the phone.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You will need a valid driver's license and a credit card in your name when you come to pick up the vehicle.

Can I add an additional driver?

Yes, additional drivers are permitted as long as they meet the same requirements as the primary driver.

Is there a minimum age for renting a car?

Yes, the minimum age for renting a car with us is 21 years. Drivers under 25 may be subject to an additional young driver fee.

Do you offer insurance coverage for the rental cars?

We offer a variety of insurance options for your rental. You can select the coverage that best suits your needs during the booking process.

Can I rent a car with a debit card instead of a credit card?

Yes, you can rent a car using a debit card. However, additional identification and proof of insurance may be required.

What is your fuel policy for rental cars?

Our standard policy is 'full-to-full'. You receive the car with a full tank and are expected to return it with a full tank.

Are there any restrictions on where I can drive the rental car?

Yes, there are restrictions. For example, driving across country borders may not be allowed or may require additional documentation and insurance.
